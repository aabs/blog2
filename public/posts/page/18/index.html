The mind boggles at the possibilities of shapelock! It is a ‘Ultra-High Molecular Weight Low Temperature Thermoplastic ' which is to say - a form of modelling plastic that can be made plyable at just above room temperature, and can be moulded to almost any shape, but which when it cools is hard as steel.
