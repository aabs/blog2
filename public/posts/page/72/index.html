Truly abstracting a persistence mechanism

The initial design that I used when I made the ancestor of norm was based upon designs by Scott Ambler - The initial intent of Ambler’s designs was definitely to provide an abstraction around the logic of a relational database. What I want to do with norm is to abstract the very notion of a data structure. When we persist an object into a row on a RDB it is almost irrelevant that the data is persistent.
